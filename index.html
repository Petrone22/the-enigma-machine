Welcome to the enigma machine! as a quick guide to get you started, the enigma machine works as follows, if you press a button on your keyboard you'll see that a different "bulb" lights up on your screen. and that's because the enigma machine is an encoding algorithm. the way it works is it takes any letter that you press on the keyboard and checks if its connected to any other letter first, via the plugboard (at the bottom), afterwards the letter travels to "the rotors", there are 3 of them. and in each one the letter you had previously pressed enters then gets converted to a new letter based on the rotor setting (it's rotation. which you can change by clicking the top or bottom letter of each rotor on top of the enigma machine) and after 3 conversions, the letter gets converted and re-enters the rotors 3 more times to ensure maximum security!
